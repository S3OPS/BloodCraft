## üéØ Expansion Methodology

Each chapter was expanded using a systematic approach:

### 1. Internal Monologue (~800-1,000 words/chapter)
- Character thoughts and emotions
- Decision-making processes
- Strategic thinking
- Memory and reflection

### 2. World-Building (~700-900 words/chapter)
- Environmental descriptions
- Sensory details (sight, sound, smell, touch, taste)
- Cultural elements
- Daily life textures

### 3. Dialogue Expansion (~700-900 words/chapter)
- Full conversations vs. summaries
- Multiple speakers in group scenes
- Physical actions during dialogue
- Strategic debates fully shown

### 4. Action Enhancement (~1,000-1,500 words/major scene)
- Combat choreography and tactics
- Physical sensations
- Strategic thinking during action
- Consequences emphasized

### 5. Subplot Development (~600-800 words/chapter)
- Romance progression
- Political maneuvering
- Personal relationships
- Secondary character arcs

### 6. Pacing Modulation (~500-700 words/chapter)
- Quiet reflection moments
- Transitional breathing space
- Character alone with thoughts
- Routine activities shown
