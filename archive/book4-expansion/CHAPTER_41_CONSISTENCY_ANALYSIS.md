## üü° MINOR ISSUE (Priority 3)

### Issue 2: Marcus's Ring Power Strain Moment

**Severity**: MINOR  
**Impact**: Extremely minor; a small inconsistency in ring mechanics that could be interpreted multiple ways  
**Category**: Magic System/World-Building Consistency

**The Problem**:
In Chapter 41, line 173 states: "The ring on my finger was hot, struggling to contain energies it was never meant to suppress to this degree."

This description suggests the ring is straining under Riven's power usage during the Devourer attack. However, in Book 3, the ring's mechanics are well-established:
- The ring suppresses Riven's Prime Archon signature/aura
- When removed, all five disciplines become visible
- The ring does NOT actively suppress Riven's use of power‚Äîit masks what he IS, not what he DOES

**Canonical Reference**:
Book 3, lines 11182-11190: When Riven removes the ring, "power blazed outward‚Äînot just crimson blood magic, but a corona of all five disciplines swirling around me. Crimson at the core, threaded with gold, blue, green-brown, and silver-white."

The ring hides his NATURE (Prime Archon status), not his POWER USAGE. During Book 3's final battle against Terravos (line 11140), Riven uses all five disciplines while still wearing the ring: "All five. All at once. A symphony of magical disciplines unified by purpose and emotion."

**Analysis**:
The current text implies the ring actively struggles to contain Riven's power output. This is a slight mischaracterization. The ring would more accurately:
- Heat up from proximity to intense magical channeling (physical effect from nearby power)
- Struggle to maintain the SUPPRESSION ILLUSION when Riven is using extreme amounts of visible multi-affinity power
- Be at risk of failing its disguise function (making the Prime Archon signature visible)

**Recommended Fix**:

Line 173 - Change:
OLD: "The ring on my finger was hot, struggling to contain energies it was never meant to suppress to this degree."
NEW: "The ring on my finger was hot, struggling to maintain its suppression as I channeled power on a scale that threatened to overwhelm even Marcus's masterwork."

OR (more accurate to ring mechanics):
NEW: "The ring on my finger was hot, its suppression faltering as I channeled multiple affinities openly‚Äîthe disguise barely holding against the torrent of Prime Archon power."

**Estimated Time**: 2 minutes  
**Priority**: Low‚Äîthis is interpretable and doesn't break immersion, but the fix would improve technical accuracy
