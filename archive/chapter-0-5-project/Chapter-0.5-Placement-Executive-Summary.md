## Literary Precedent (This Works)

**Stories that reveal backstory AFTER character introduction but BEFORE emotional turning point:**

1. **Harry Potter** - Snape's backstory (Book 7, after years of mystery)
   - Result: Transforms entire series understanding

2. **Avatar: The Last Airbender** - Zuko's backstory (Episode 12)
   - Result: Transforms villain into tragic hero

3. **The Witcher** - Yennefer's transformation (After meeting adult version)
   - Result: Harsh personality becomes heartbreaking

**Pattern:** The most impactful backstories are revealed when they create IMMEDIATE emotional resonance with current events.

**That's exactly what Chapter 0.5 does for the catgirl reveal.**
