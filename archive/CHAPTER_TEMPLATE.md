## Required Elements Checklist

### All Chapters Must Include
- [ ] Character development moment
- [ ] Plot progression
- [ ] At least one of: action scene, political maneuvering, or romance scene
- [ ] Consistent character voices
- [ ] Clear scene transitions
- [ ] World-building detail (not info-dumping)
- [ ] Supporting character interaction
- [ ] Emotional beats
- [ ] Tension or conflict

### Chapter Balance (Aim for)
- [ ] 40% Romance/relationship development
- [ ] 30% Action/conflict
- [ ] 20% Politics/world-building
- [ ] 10% Introspection/character growth

### Quality Standards
- [ ] Spell-check completed
- [ ] Grammar review done
- [ ] Character voices consistent
- [ ] Timeline matches previous chapters
- [ ] World-building rules maintained
- [ ] Pacing feels appropriate
- [ ] Dialogue sounds natural
- [ ] Descriptions are vivid but not excessive

## Example Filled Template

### Chapter Information
- **Chapter Number**: 31
- **Chapter Title**: "Shadows of the Past"
- **Version**: Paradox
- **Word Count Target**: 4,500 words
- **Arc/Phase**: Book 2, Act 1 - Aftermath of Revelation

### Opening
**Scene Setting**: Nocturne, Riven's chambers, dawn after the revelation
**Hook**: Riven wakes from a dream that he now knows was a memory
**Emotional State**: Riven is wrestling with his dual identity; Raechelle is cautiously hopeful

### Rising Action
**Key Events**:
1. Riven confronts ancient memories bleeding through
2. Conversation with Raechelle about who he was vs. who he is
3. External faction reacts to his revealed identity

**Character Interactions**: Deep conversation between Riven and Raechelle about trust and redemption

**Conflict**: Riven struggles with accepting his past while choosing his future

**World-Building**: Exploration of reincarnation mechanics and soul continuity

### Climax
**Key Moment**: Riven declares he will not be defined by his past
**Emotional Peak**: Raechelle reveals her fears about whether she's loving him or the memory of who he was

### Resolution
**Immediate Aftermath**: They recommit to building something new together
**Setup**: External faction arrives demanding answers
**Character Reflection**: Riven realizes redemption is a daily choice, not a single moment
