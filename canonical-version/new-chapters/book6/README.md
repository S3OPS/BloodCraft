## Critical Elements to Execute

### Essence Blade Mechanics
- Clear rules for how it works
- Consistent cost (power loss not death)
- Visual/experiential descriptions

### Three-Way Consciousness Merge
- Distinct from normal telepathy
- Each character's memories/perspectives accessible
- Unified purpose while maintaining identities
- Specific sensory descriptions

### Power Loss Aftermath
- Riven: Five affinities → Two weak affinities
- Elena: Full Prime Archon → Reduced Prime Archon
- Zephyra: Ancient power → Fraction remaining
- Consistent portrayal of limitations
- Character adaptation and acceptance

### Enhanced Devourer
- Intelligent, strategic, learning entity
- Prime Archon hunting abilities
- Reality manipulation across dimensions
- Threatening but ultimately beatable

### Emotional Beats
- Each Prime Archon contemplating solo sacrifice
- Family goodbyes before final battle
- Merged consciousness experience
- Waking after sacrifice
- Five-year epilogue normalcy
- Final family scene completion
