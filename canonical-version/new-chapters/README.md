# New Chapters - Raechelle's POV

This directory contains additional chapters written from Raechelle Darkpurr's point of view, adding a distinct woman's perspective to the Blood Craft narrative.

**Current Status**: Eight complete Raechelle POV chapters spanning the entire narrative arc, from the initial awakening through the final confrontation with Terravos.

## Purpose

The canonical version of Blood Craft is primarily told from Riven's perspective. These chapters provide:

- **Raechelle's inner thoughts and feelings** during key moments in the story
- **A distinct woman's POV** on the relationship and events
- **Deeper insight into the familiar bond** from the familiar's perspective
- **Additional emotional depth** to pivotal scenes
- **Balance to the narrative** through alternating perspectives

## Chapter Structure

These chapters are numbered with ".5" to indicate they are interstitial chapters that fit between existing chapters. They should be read in sequence with the main story for the full dual-POV experience.

## Writing Style

These chapters maintain consistency with the main narrative while offering:

- **First-person POV** from Raechelle's perspective
- **Deeper introspection** on emotions and centuries of experience
- **Feminine perspective** on the relationship dynamics
- **Unique insights** into supernatural life and familiar bonds
- **Complementary information** to Riven's POV chapters

## Integration with Main Story

These chapters are designed to:

- **Enhance, not replace** the existing narrative
- **Provide new information** without contradicting established canon
- **Deepen emotional resonance** of key scenes
- **Offer context** for Raechelle's actions and decisions
- **Create a more balanced narrative** with both perspectives
