# New Chapters - Raechelle's POV

This directory contains additional chapters written from Raechelle Darkpurr's point of view, adding a distinct woman's perspective to the Blood Craft narrative.

## Purpose

The canonical version of Blood Craft is primarily told from Riven's perspective. These chapters provide:

- **Raechelle's inner thoughts and feelings** during key moments in the story
- **A distinct woman's POV** on the relationship and events
- **Deeper insight into the familiar bond** from the familiar's perspective
- **Additional emotional depth** to pivotal scenes
- **Balance to the narrative** through alternating perspectives

## Chapter Structure

These chapters are numbered with ".5" to indicate they are interstitial chapters that fit between existing chapters. They should be read in sequence with the main story for the full dual-POV experience.

### Chapter 2.5 - The Vigil Ends
**Placement**: After Chapter 2 (The Accident)

Raechelle's perspective on:
- The moment she felt Riven's power awaken
- Her desperate rush to reach him
- Finding the aftermath of the accident
- Her two centuries of preparation for this moment
- Her first real encounter with Riven and the overwhelming bond

**Key Themes**: Duty vs. desire, centuries of waiting, the familiar's burden

---

### Chapter 7.5 - On the Eve of Forever
**Placement**: After Chapter 7 (Preparation), before Chapter 8 (Bonding Ritual)

Raechelle's perspective on:
- The night before the bonding ritual
- Her fears about falling in love with her charge
- Memories of previous bonds and their failures
- Her conversation with Riven about choosing each other
- The difference between duty and love

**Key Themes**: Love vs. professional distance, fear of loss, choosing to be vulnerable

---

### Chapter 15.5 - When Darkness Descends
**Placement**: After Chapter 15 (The Crimson Ball)

Raechelle's perspective on:
- The earth elemental attack at the Crimson Ball
- Her protective instincts overriding everything else
- Fighting alongside Riven through their bond
- Being injured and Riven's terrifying response
- The realization that love is both weakness and strength

**Key Themes**: Protection, partnership in battle, the cost of love in war

---

### Chapter 29.5 - The Weight of Centuries
**Placement**: After Chapter 29 (Revelations in Ruins)

Raechelle's perspective on:
- Reflecting on six years with Riven
- The difference between this bond and her previous ones
- Facing the final confrontation with Terravos
- Hope for their future together
- The promise of forever

**Key Themes**: Reflection, hope, the meaning of forever, family planning
