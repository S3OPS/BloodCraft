## üé® Creative Guidelines

### Character Development - Riven

**Starting Point**:
- 23-year-old college student
- Ordinary life, unaware of heritage
- Driven by grief and desire for justice

**Arc Direction**:
- Grief ‚Üí Purpose
- Powerless ‚Üí Powerful
- Isolated ‚Üí Leader
- Uncertain ‚Üí Confident

**Key Traits to Maintain**:
- Protective nature
- Intelligence and strategic thinking
- Moral compass (clear right and wrong)
- Capacity for love and vulnerability
- Growth through challenges

**Avoid**:
- Moral ambiguity or questionable choices
- Excessive angst or self-doubt (beyond natural growth)
- Becoming too dark or cynical
- Losing his essential heroism

### Character Development - Raechelle

**Role**: Love interest, mentor, powerful ally

**Key Traits**:
- Strong, independent Blood Mage
- Supportive but not subservient
- Genuine emotional connection with Riven
- Own power and agency
- Trustworthy and loyal

**Arc Direction**:
- Independent warrior ‚Üí Equal partner
- Cautious ‚Üí Deeply committed
- Mentor ‚Üí Lover and ally

**Dom/Sub Dynamic**:
- Consensual and healthy
- Built on trust and communication
- Serves character connection, not just titillation
- Shows vulnerability and strength in both roles

### Relationship Development

**Phase 1 (Chapters 1-10)**: Initial attraction and bonding
- Chemistry and sexual tension
- Mutual respect and admiration
- Beginning of emotional intimacy
- Establishing trust

**Phase 2 (Chapters 11-20)**: Deepening connection
- Exploration of Dom/sub dynamic
- Vulnerability and emotional openness
- Tested by external conflicts
- Growing commitment

**Phase 3 (Chapters 21-30)**: Mature partnership
- Deep trust and intimacy
- Facing ultimate challenges together
- Mutual support in crisis
- Commitment to shared future

## üìù Chapter Development Framework

### Standard Chapter Structure

**Opening** (10-15%):
- Hook or continuation from previous chapter
- Establish scene and emotional state

**Rising Action** (60-70%):
- Character interactions
- Plot advancement
- Conflict or tension
- World-building elements

**Climax** (10-15%):
- Chapter's key moment
- Emotional or action peak
- Decision point or revelation

**Resolution** (5-10%):
- Immediate aftermath
- Setup for next chapter
- Character reflection

### Required Elements Per Chapter

‚úÖ **Must Have**:
- Character development moment
- Plot progression
- At least one of: action, politics, or romance scene
- Consistent character voices
- Clear scene transitions

‚úÖ **Should Have**:
- World-building detail
- Supporting character interaction
- Emotional beats
- Tension or conflict

‚ö†Ô∏è **Avoid**:
- Info-dumping
- Stagnant pacing
- Out-of-character dialogue
- Repetitive action sequences
- Unclear transitions

## ‚öîÔ∏è Action & Combat Guidelines

### Combat Scenes
- Clear choreography (reader can visualize)
- Show supernatural powers in action
- Maintain stakes (consequences matter)
- Balance action with character emotion
- Varied tactics and approaches

### Power Progression
- Gradual and earned growth
- New abilities introduced with purpose
- Training and practice shown
- Limits and costs maintained
- No sudden unexplained power-ups
