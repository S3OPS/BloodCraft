## Story Framework

### Core Conflict
The Devourer, an ancient reality-consuming entity, is awakening because two Prime Archons (Riven and Zephyra) exist simultaneously—something that should never happen. The universe rebels against this imbalance, and if not stopped, the Devourer will consume all magic, starting with the supernatural world.

### Protagonist's Dilemma
Riven must choose between:
1. **Death**: Sacrifice himself to restore balance (unacceptable)
2. **Stasis**: Enter suspended animation for millennia (loses family forever)
3. **Fight**: Work with Zephyra to destroy the Devourer (likely suicide mission)

### Stakes
- **Personal**: Elena's future, Raechelle's happiness, family unity
- **Political**: Supernatural community stability, Riven's role as the Balance
- **Cosmic**: Existence of magic itself, reality integrity
- **Temporal**: Only weeks before point of no return

### Character Arcs Established
- **Riven**: From hidden Prime Archon to accepting his true power
- **Elena**: Coming of age through impossible crisis
- **Raechelle**: Supporting partner facing potential loss
- **Zephyra**: Woman out of time with nothing to lose
