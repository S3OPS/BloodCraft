# Blood Craft: Paradox Writing - Completion Summary

## Project Overview
Implemented the "Blood Archon's Paradox" alternate storyline by writing Chapters 21-30 of the Blood Craft narrative, featuring the major twist that Riven Sixxx is the reincarnation of the ancient "mad" Blood Archon.

## Chapters Completed

### Phase 1: Chapters 21-24 (The Breaking)
